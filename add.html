Add a new recipe

Upload a recipe from another website to store it in the MyRecipes app

Upon submitting, you will be redirected to an external provider. It may take up to 24 hours for the submitted recipe to be processed and added to your 'My Recipes' page.

Once uploaded and the confirmation message is shown, tap the 'Done' button in the top left corner of your screen to return to the homepage.

If a link is submitted that is not a recipe, it will most likely be disapproved and not added to your library.
