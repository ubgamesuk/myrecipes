Chopped Greek Salad

Ingredients

- romaine lettuce, chopped - red onion, cut into rings - kalamata olives - plum tomatoes, coarsely chopped - ground black pepper - lemon, juiced - oregano - olive oil - green pepper, chopped - dried basil - red wine vinegar - cucumbers - salt - crumbled feta cheese

Directions

1.In a salad bowl, mix all the vegetables and cheese together. 2.In a separate bowl, prepare the salad's dressing by stirring all the spices together: olive oil, lemon juice, oregano, basil, red wine vinegar, salt and pepper. If you want the dressing to be smoother, you can use a blender. 3.Pour the dressing over the salad bowl and toss to combine.
