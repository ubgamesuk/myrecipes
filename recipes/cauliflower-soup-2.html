Cauliflower Soup 2

Ingredients

- knob of butter - crushed clove of garlic - ground nutmeg - black pepper - salt - head cauliflower, chopped - large diced carrot - chopped spring onions

Directions

1.In a large pot over medium heat, melt butter. 2.Cook garlic in butter 30 seconds, then stir in nutmeg, pepper and salt and cook 30 seconds more. 3.Pour in the water and introduce the cauliflower. 4.Bring to the boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer 20 minutes until cauliflower is tender. 5.In a small saucepan over medium heat, cook carrot with water to cover until just tender. 6.Drain and reserve. 7.Pur√©e cauliflower soup in a blender or food processor or with an immersion blender. 8.Stir in reserved carrots, spring onion and parsley. 9.Serve.
