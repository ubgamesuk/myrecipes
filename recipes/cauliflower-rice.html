Cauliflower Rice

Ingredients

- large head cauliflower


Directions

1.Wash and thoroughly dry cauliflower, then remove all greens and cut into 4 even sections. 2.With a box grater, use the medium-sized holes, or a food processor with the grater attachment, to grate the cauliflower into the size of rice, leaving any large, tough stems behind. 3.Transfer to a clean towel or paper towel and press to remove any excess moisture, which can make your dish soggy. 4.Once you have your cauliflower rice, it's easy to cook! Simply saut√© in a large skillet over medium heat in 1 Tbsp oil. 5.Cover with a lid so the cauliflower steams and becomes more tender. 6.Cook for a total of 5-8 minutes, then season as desired (such as with soy sauce or salt and pepper). 7.Use cauliflower rice in recipes that call for rice, such as stir fries fesenjan or fried rice.
