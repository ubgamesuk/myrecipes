Bombay Potatoes

Ingredients

- Jersey Royals or new potatoes 800g, halved or quartered - cloves, chopped - chopped ginger (thumb sized) - 4 chopped plum tomatoes - vegetable oil - cumin seeds - ground turmeric - thinly sliced chilli - garam masala (1 tsp) - coriander, finely chopped

Directions

1.Cook the potatoes in a large pan of lightly salted boiling water until tender to the point of a knife, then drain well. 2.Tip the garlic and ginger into a blender with 4 tbsp of water then whizz until smooth. 3.Add the tomatoes and whizz again briefly. 4.Heat the vegetable oil in a frying pan over a medium-high heat and fry the cooked potatoes for 10-15 minutes, tossing now and again, until really crispy. 5.Turn down the heat and add the cumin and black mustard seeds, and the ground turmeric, and fry for a minute. 6.Tip in the garlic, ginger and tomato pur√©e with three quarters of the red chilli, and cook gently for 10 minutes until thickened. 7.Stir in the garam masala and season well. 8.Tip into a serving dish and top with the coriander leaves, remaining red chilli and lime wedges for squeezing over.
