Chunky Guacamole

Ingredients

- jalapeno peppers - avocados - red onion, diced - juiced lemons - minced garlic cloves - chopped coriander - black pepper - chopped cherry tomatoes - salt - ground cumin

Directions

1.Cut the avocados across the center and split in half. 2.Using a large spoon, take the pit out of the avocados. 3.Place 2 avocados in a bowl and using a large spoon, break up the avocados, but don't mash them too much. 4.Stir in the juice of 1 lemon, this will also help prevent the avocados from browning. 5.Add the remaining ingredients. 6.Give it all a good mix and season to taste.
