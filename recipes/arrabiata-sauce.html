Classic Arrabbiata Sauce

Ingredients

- olive oil - chopped onion - garlic cloves - red wine - caster sugar - fresh basil - crushed chillies - tomato puree - lemon juice - Italian herb seasoning - black pepper - 400g chopped tomatoes - chopped fresh parsley

Directions

1.Heat oil in a large frying pan over medium heat. 2.Saute onion and garlic in oil for 5 minutes. 3.Stir in wine, sugar, basil, crushed chillies, tomato puree, lemon juice, Italian herb seasoning, black pepper and tomatoes; bring to the boil. 4.Reduce heat to medium and simmer uncovered about 15 minutes. 5.Stir in parsley. 6.Ladle over the hot cooked pasta of your choice.
